About

Hi! I'm Liz. President of a successful start-up, Gallup Certified Strengths Coach, Associate Certified Coach (ACC), and a mom of six. I know what a full life looks like — and I know what it takes to thrive inside of one.

I'm a firm believer that people who live and use their strengths are happier and more productive — at home and at work. One of my personal talents? Human Tetris. I love discovering what makes people light up, what drains them, and then rearranging the pieces so everything fits better.

Amplify Light was born from a simple belief: that even in the hardest seasons, we can choose radical hope. Not the naive kind that ignores the pain, but the fierce, intentional kind that says "I will bloom here."

For Leaders

Strengths-based coaching that helps you lead from who you actually are.

Strengths Coaching

Everyone has a combination of strengths that is unique to them. The problem isn't that you don't have them — it's that no one ever helped you see them clearly and put them to work.

As a Gallup Certified Strengths Coach, I take a holistic approach that supports both your career and your life. Each area of happiness improves your ability to show up in another.

What coaching with me looks like:

  • Discover the strengths you're already using — and the ones you're leaving on the table
  • Get clear on what drains you and design your way out of it
  • Build a practical plan that honors both your ambitions and your wellbeing
  • Walk away with tools you'll actually use — not theory you'll forget
